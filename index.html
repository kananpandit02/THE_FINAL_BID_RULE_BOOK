Event Overview

THE FINAL BID is a strategic cricket auction simulation event designed to evaluate participants on decision-making, analytical thinking, budget management, risk handling, and intelligent squad-building.

Each team represents a professional cricket franchise and participates in a live, offline auction to assemble a competitive squad from a fixed player pool. The event closely follows real-world league auction rules and dynamics.

Player Pool Rules

  • Total Players in Auction Pool: 140
  • 100 Indian players and 40 Overseas players
  • Each player is assigned a fixed Base Price and Base Points
  • Players are categorized into:
    • Batsman
    • Bowler
    • All-rounder
    • Wicketkeeper
  • Player roles, prices, and points are final and non-negotiable
  • No new players will be added once the auction starts
  • Player data can be viewed only through the official auction system

Auction & Bidding Rules

  • Each team starts with a virtual purse of ‚Çπ90 Crore
  • The auction is conducted by an official auctioneer
  • One player is auctioned at a time
  • The auctioneer announces the player name, role, and base price
  • The first bid must be placed within 5 seconds, otherwise the player is declared UNSOLD
  • After the first bid, each subsequent bid must be placed within 3 seconds
  • The countdown resets after every valid bid
  • Jump bidding is allowed as per auctioneer instructions
  • Once declared UNSOLD, the player will NOT be re-auctioned
  • A team cannot place a bid if:
    • Budget becomes insufficient
    • Squad composition rules are violated
  • The auctioneer‚Äôs decision is final and binding

Squad Composition Rules

  • Each team must build a squad of exactly 15 players
  • Minimum squad requirements:
    • 5 Batsmen
    • 4 Bowlers
    • 3 All-rounders
    • 2 Wicketkeepers
  • Maximum 7 Overseas players allowed in the squad
  • Teams must manage budget carefully to complete the squad
  • Failure to meet squad requirements results in DISQUALIFICATION

Playing XI Rules

  • Each team must submit a Playing XI of exactly 11 players
  • Playing XI must include:
    • Minimum 3 Batsmen
    • Minimum 3 Bowlers
    • Minimum 2 All-rounders
    • Minimum 1 Wicketkeeper
  • Maximum 4 Overseas players allowed in Playing XI
  • Any Playing XI violating the above rules will be declared INVALID

Scoring & Tie-Breakers

  • Total team score is calculated as: Sum of Base Points of the Playing XI
  • The team with the highest total score is declared the WINNER
  • In case of a tie, the following tie-breakers are applied in order:
    1. Higher remaining budget after auction
    2. Fewer overseas players in Playing XI
    3. Faster completion of full squad

Team Cart

  • Monitor remaining budget in real time

Event Lead & Coordination

The smooth execution, fairness, and integrity of THE FINAL BID are ensured by the official Event Lead and Coordinators appointed by the organizing committee.

  • Event Lead:
    Responsible for overall event management, rule enforcement, and final decisions.
  • Auctioneer:
    Conducts the live auction, manages bidding flow, time limits, and declares SOLD / UNSOLD status.
  • Technical Coordinator:
    Manages the official player pool system, scoring logic, and data accuracy.
  • Judging Panel:
    Verifies squad validity, Playing XI compliance, scoring, and tie-breakers.
  • Authority Clause:
    Decisions taken by the Event Lead, Auctioneer, and Judges are final and binding. No disputes will be entertained once a decision is announced.
